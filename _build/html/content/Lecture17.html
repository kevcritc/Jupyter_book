2. Protein Structure Hierarchy#

Proteins are built from amino acids, and their function depends on the structure:

  • Linear sequence of amino acids

  • Linked by peptide bonds (between -COOH and -NH‚ÇÇ)

Secondary Structure:#

  • Local folding: Œ±-helices and Œ≤-sheets

  • Full 3D shape of a single chain

    • Disulfide bridges

  • Multiple polypeptide chains (subunits) form a larger complex

What Is Nanomedicine?#

  • Application of nanotechnology in medicine for:

    • Prevention

    • Diagnosis

    • Treatment of diseases

  • Interdisciplinary field combining:

    • Physics, Biology, Chemistry, Engineering

    • Mathematics, Computer Science, Medicine

Emerging Fields:#

  • Nanotherapeutics

  • Molecularly targeted medicine

  • Immunotherapy

  • Synthetic biology

  • Lab-on-chip systems

  • Pre-clinical testing of nanoparticles

  • Genetic and tissue engineering

Why Use Nanoparticles in Medicine?#

  • Size: 1‚Äì100 nm (same scale as biomolecules)

  • Small enough to:

    • Interact with proteins, DNA

    • Enter cells and tissues

  • Used for:

    • Drug delivery

    • Imaging

    • Diagnostics
